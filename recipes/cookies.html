Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 3 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 1 and 1/4 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) package of semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda together in a medium bowl, and set aside.
  2. Beat butter and sugars together in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment on medium-high speed until light and fluffy, about 5 minutes. Add eggs one at a time, scraping down sides of bowl as necessary. Beat for an additional 3 minutes. Add vanilla and beat for 10 seconds.
  3. Reduce speed to low and gradually add in the flour mixture, blending just until combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop 1 1/2-inch balls of dough 3 inches apart onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet; freeze for 15 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (225 degrees C).
  6. Bake cookies in the preheated oven, rotating halfway through, until golden around the edges and slightly gooey inside, about 8 minutes. Let stand on the baking sheet for 4 to 5 minutes, then transfer to a cooling rack.

Recipe Tip: This is a great base recipe that can accommodate other mix-ins!
